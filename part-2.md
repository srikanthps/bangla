# Part 2 – Your First 100 Words

# Welcome

Congratulations!

You can now pronounce Bangla words using Roman letters. In this lesson, you'll begin building the vocabulary needed for everyday conversations.

Rather than memorizing words alphabetically, you'll learn them by topic—the same way children naturally acquire language.

Don't try to memorize all 100 words in one sitting.

Instead:

- Learn 15–20 words at a time.
- Say every word aloud.
- Use the practice sentences.
- Review the words regularly.

By the end of this lesson, you'll know enough vocabulary to understand and create dozens of simple sentences.
